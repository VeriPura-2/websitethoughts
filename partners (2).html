Three reasons to partner.

Questions

What forwarders and carriers
ask us most.

Do we need to be a compliance expert to sell VeriPura?
No. VeriPura handles all the compliance intelligence. Your role as a partner is to introduce the service to clients and position it as part of your offering. We provide sales materials, client-facing one-pagers, and joint demo support. You don't need to understand the regulatory detail — that's what VeriPura does.
What corridors does VeriPura currently cover?
VeriPura operates on any corridor where institutional integrity risk creates documentary compliance exposure — primarily origins with a Corruption Perceptions Index score between 25–45. Current focus corridors include Thailand, Vietnam, Indonesia, India, Brazil, Mexico, and the Philippines into EU and US markets. New corridors are added continuously based on partner and client demand.
Is there exclusivity available?
Yes. Anchor partners — those who commit early and meet volume targets — can negotiate geographic or segment exclusivity. For example, an anchor partner focused on Dutch SME importers sourcing from Southeast Asia can discuss exclusivity for that segment. Exclusivity terms are negotiated individually and included in the partner agreement.
What happens if a client we introduce goes directly to VeriPura?
Clients introduced through the channel programme are tagged to your partner account at the point of introduction. If a client subsequently contacts VeriPura directly, your attribution is maintained and your revenue share continues for the life of that client relationship, subject to the terms of your partner agreement.
We already use a TMS / freight platform — does VeriPura integrate?
VeriPura connects via REST API to existing TMS, ERP, and document management systems. For partner organisations, we prioritise API integration as part of the onboarding process so that compliance validation fits into your existing workflow rather than adding a parallel system. Integration scope and timeline are scoped during onboarding.
