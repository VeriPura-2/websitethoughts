How It Works

From client shipment to compliance record
in five steps.

When your importer client has a shipment ready to move, you submit their document bundle through VeriPura. The check runs against the destination market's specific requirements. You receive a verdict — and a blockchain-attested record — before the container departs.

01
Client submits document bundle
Phytosanitary certificates, health certificates, certificates of origin, certificates of analysis, FDA Prior Notice, and any other documents required for the destination market.
02
VeriPura maps corridor and frameworks
HS code identification, origin/destination corridor, and applicable regulatory regimes — EU 2017/625, FDA FSMA, EUDR, TRACES NT, FSMA 204, UK FRCR — are identified automatically.
03
AI compliance check runs across all documents
50+ specialised agents check every field — authority signatures, product codes, establishment numbers, certificate validity dates, EUDR due diligence records — against the requirements of the destination market.
04
Verdict delivered with remediation guidance
Pass, fail, or fix. Any flagged issues include specific remediation instructions — so errors are corrected at the point when they are still fixable: before the container moves.
05
Blockchain-attested compliance record issued
Every check is anchored to the IOTA blockchain, producing an immutable, timestamped audit trail. You provide this to your importer client as part of the shipment dossier — and retain it as documented evidence of compliance diligence.
